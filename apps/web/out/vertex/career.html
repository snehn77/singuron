Career Assistant

Local-first AI job-search intelligence that knows what it knows.

An end-to-end job-search co-pilot. It discovers roles, scores them honestly with a confidence layer, tailors your CV without fabricating anything, and tracks the whole pipeline, entirely on your machine.

Smart job discovery

Zero-LLM-cost scanning across 90+ companies via Greenhouse, Ashby, and Lever, deduplicated into a single pipeline.

AI evaluation & scoring

Six-block deep analysis of role fit, compensation, growth, and culture, with a confidence badge on every score.

Grounding gate

An anti-fabrication safety valve: every claim in a generated CV is traced back to your profile, and ungrounded claims are blocked.

Tracker & activity heatmap

A full pipeline view with scores, statuses, and a GitHub-style heatmap of your daily application activity.

CV tailoring & auto-fill

A custom resume per application plus a Chrome extension that fills Greenhouse, Lever, and Workday forms, but never clicks submit.

Local-first & private

No cloud and no telemetry. Bring your own Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini key and keep every byte on your machine.
